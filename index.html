About Me

I enjoy working with technology and finding solutions to technical problems that help systems run more effectively. I'm naturally curious and like learning how different systems fit together, which allows me to approach issues carefully and logically. I pay close attention to detail and take pride in doing my work properly and reliably.

I also enjoy working through more challenging technical problems and taking the time to understand new technologies as they come up. I see learning as an ongoing process and aim to continually improve my skills by thinking problems through and building practical experience.

  • Naturally curious about technology and how systems fit together.
  • Detail-oriented and logical — I take pride in reliable, well-crafted work.
  • Always learning, quick to pick up new tools and technologies.
  • Building practical experience by working through real problems carefully.
